Our Product

Arthro-Robotic Mechanism (ARM) is designed with a 4-point rotating mechanism that allows for precise control to the thousandth of a millimeter. World first technology has allowed ARM to be one of the safest robots in the world, and this will allow flawless surgical procedures and perfect results.

Simple User Experience

ARM is built with the user in mind. We have created a simple and intuitive user experience for a quick and easy setup process, and controls that are straightforward and direct. Minimizing confusion and conflicts are important for safe operation.

Safety

ARM utilizes AI to stabilize the robot and operate on the patient. ARM takes advantage of a neural network to recognize the relative positions of its arms and where the problem in the patient is. Should any error occur while ARM is mid-procedure, the operator will be alerted immediately, and ARM will go into safety mode in order to ensure that no harm is done to the patient while the error gets sorted out.

(While in safe mode ARM will give partial control to the operator and ensure that the patient remains safe at all times.)
